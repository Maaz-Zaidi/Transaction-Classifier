# Archived: Original 15-Category Schema
**Archived:** 2026-03-26
**Reason:** I'm adopting mitulshah/transaction-categorization dataset's 10 categories as starting point since they're much more feasible rather then implementing four new categories for which I don't have data for. These original categories are preserved for future reference when I extend/remap.

---

1. **Food** — vending machines, restaurants, fast food, cafes, bakeries
2. **Groceries** — supermarkets, Costco, Freshco
3. **Housing** — Rent, Mortgage
4. **Transportation** — Gas, Parking, OC Transpo, Uber, Presto
5. **Retail** — Sale of goods; clothing, furniture, malls, products
6. **Entertainment** — Digital goods (movies, games); experiences (cinema, concert)
7. **Services** — Barber, house cleaning, car maintenance/repair
8. **Health** — Pharmacy, dental, vision
9. **Utilities** — Phone, internet (Fido, Rogers), hydro, water, gas (home)
10. **Subscriptions** — Goodlife, ChatGPT, AWS, Amazon Prime
11. **Education** — University tuition, textbooks, loan repayments, school payments
12. **E-Transfers** — Any money sent outwards/inwards (Interac)
13. **Banking** — Credit payments, savings installments, non-education loans
14. **Income** — Payroll, refunds, deposits
15. **Misc** — Anything unclassifiable

## Gap
The mitulshah dataset has: Food & Dining, Transportation, Shopping & Retail, Entertainment & Recreation, Healthcare & Medical, Utilities & Services, Financial Services, Income, Government & Legal, Charity & Donations

**Missing from dataset:** Groceries (merged into Food), Subscriptions, E-Transfers, Banking, Education, Misc
**In dataset but not original:** Government & Legal, Charity & Donations
